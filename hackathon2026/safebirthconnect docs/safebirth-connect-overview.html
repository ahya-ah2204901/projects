The Problem

In displacement camps and crisis settings, pregnant women face critical barriers to receiving timely care

No Internet Access

WiFi and mobile data are unreliable or completely unavailable in most crisis settings

Basic Phones Only

Many women only have access to feature phones—no smartphones or apps

Overwhelmed Systems

Existing health infrastructure is absent, informal, or completely overwhelmed

Uncoordinated Help

Local volunteers (midwives, experienced mothers) exist but aren't connected or organized

💡 The Insight

SMS works when nothing else does. It requires no internet, works on any phone, and is familiar to everyone. We can build a complete coordination system on top of simple text messages.

The Solution

A hybrid SMS + Optional App system that coordinates maternal care through local volunteers

🌍 Arabic + English support built-in from day one
1

Registration via SMS

Pregnant women and volunteers register by sending a simple structured SMS. No app required, no internet needed.

REG MOTHER
CAMP A
ZONE 3
DUE 15-02
RISK HIGH
2

Help Request

When a mother needs assistance—whether emergency or routine support—she sends a simple SMS command.

EMERGENCY HELP
— or —
SUPPORT REQUEST
3

Smart Matching

System automatically matches based on: zone proximity, volunteer certification level, availability status, and urgency.

4

Multi-Volunteer Alert

Multiple nearby volunteers receive SMS alerts simultaneously. First to respond accepts the case—others get auto-closure notification.

🚨 EMERGENCY Zone 3
Risk: HIGH | Due: 2 days
Reply ACCEPT 4721 to respond
5

Confirmation Loop

Both mother and volunteer receive confirmation. Case is tracked until closed.

User Roles

Three distinct user types with carefully scoped permissions

👩

Pregnant Women

  • Register via SMS (any phone)
  • Request emergency or routine help
  • Identified by unique phone number
  • Optional app for easier registration
🤝

Volunteers

  • Certified (midwife, nurse) or community
  • Receive alerts via SMS
  • Accept/decline cases
  • Offline case inbox in app
  • View dashboards when connected
🏥

NGO Coordinators

  • Dashboard in Flutter app
  • Read-only aggregated data
  • Zone coverage heat maps
  • Demand trends & gaps
  • Inform volunteer deployment
